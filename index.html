Our Purpose


ALPFA (Association of Latino Professional For America) is the longest standing Latino organization with 72,000+
members assembled in 44 professional and more than 160 student chapters across America. Our vision is to be the
premier business organization for expanding opportunities for Latino leadership in the global market.
